Web Developer & iOS Developer from the northwest of England

Development

I class myself as a full stack web developer, I can build backend and frontend. I have experience using Wordpress, Laravel and Drupal and I specalise in CSS framework development.

I also develop iOS applications in my spare time and currently have a iPhone app on the AppStore with another under development.

Academic

I have a first class degree in Computer Network Technology BSc. I was also awarded the British computer society student of the year award.

Things I studied at University include Penetration Testing, Network Design, Forensics, Cisco CCNA & Network Protocols.

Interests

My Interests are varied, I play games and I currently play on the PC and PS4. My favourite games include Call Of Duty, Battlefield 4, Guild Wars 2 & Minecraft.

I play the Guitar and Bass and whenever I can, I hit some balls on the golf course.
